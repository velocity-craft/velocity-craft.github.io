Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Powerful tools that stay out of your way until the moment you need them.

📋

Smart Templates

Reusable templates with auto-fill variables — date, time, location, clipboard, and interactive input fields. Adapted to your locale.

⚡

Snippets

Pin frequently used phrases to the editor toolbar for one-tap insertion. Addresses, signatures, boilerplate — always within reach.

🖼

Image Notes

Attach images with a full-screen viewer. Pinch to zoom, swipe to dismiss. Write rich markdown memos alongside your images.

⏰

Inline Reminders

Type @@ to set a reminder right inside your note. It appears as a visual chip — no app-switching required.

📁

Folders & Tags

Organize with folders, subfolders, and tags. Star, pin, archive, or trash. Customize your sidebar to match your workflow.

❄

Cold Storage

Move old notes to a separate storage space to keep your active workspace fast and lightweight. Access anytime with biometric unlock.

☁

Backup & Sync

Local compressed backups, Markdown file export, Dropbox cloud backup, and cross-device sync via Google Sign-In.

🔍

Focus & Find

Minimize distractions with Focus Mode. Locate and replace text across long documents with Find & Replace.

📤

Export Anywhere

Share your work as Markdown, PDF, or plain text. Your notes, your format, your choice.
